# The Best Restaurants In The University District - Seattle

## Restaurant Listings

Hey Bagel
- Address: 4610 Village Ct NE, Seattle, Washington 98105
- Price: $$$$
- Cuisine: Bagels
- Perfect For: Breakfast, Serious Takeout Operation
- Rating: 8.7
- Description: Former pop-up now permanent fixture featuring "shiny shell across the top, a resounding crunch, and a spongy center of bready fluff." Diverse schmear options available.

Saint Bread
- Address: 1421 NE Boat St, Seattle, WA 98105
- Price: $$$$
- Cuisine: Bakery/Cafe
- Perfect For: Breakfast, Brunch, Kids, Cheap Eats, Serious Takeout Operation
- Rating: 7.8
- Description: Rustic waterfront setting serving za'atar avocado toast, Norwegian school buns, and standout breakfast sandwiches on Japanese melonpan.

Hinoki
- Address: 1421 NE Boat St, Seattle, Seattle 98105
- Price: $$$$
- Cuisine: Japanese
- Perfect For: Drinks & A Light Bite, Casual Dinners, Sitting Outside
- Rating: 8.2
- Description: Seasonal truck operation featuring sweet vermouth spritzes, chicken karaage, roasted maitakes, and bacon candy.

Din Tai Fung
- Address: 2621 NE 46th St, Seattle, WA 98105
- Price: $$$$
- Cuisine: Chinese
- Perfect For: Lunch, Big Groups, Kids
- Rating: 8.4
- Description: Massive steamy dining room known for exceptional xiao long bao dumplings. Expect 45-minute waits.

Korean Tofu House
- Address: 4142 Brooklyn Ave NE, Seattle, WA 98105
- Price: $$$$
- Cuisine: Korean
- Perfect For: Casual Dinners, Lunch, Dining Solo, Serious Takeout Operation
- Rating: 8.0
- Description: Basement location featuring sizzling short ribs, kimchi tofu stew, and banchan sides.

Little Duck
- Address: 4100 Roosevelt Way NE, Seattle, WA 98105
- Price: $$$$
- Cuisine: Chinese
- Perfect For: Casual Dinners, Lunch, Walk-Ins, Being Left Alone
- Rating: 8.1
- Description: Northeastern Chinese specialist with fried peppercorn chicken and excellent pork dumplings.

Thai Tom
- Address: 4543 University Way NE, Seattle, WA 98105
- Price: $$$$
- Cuisine: Thai
- Perfect For: Dining Solo, Gluten-Free Options, Lunch, Serious Takeout Operation
- Description: Cash-only establishment with consistent lines. "Noodles are always wonderfully saucy with some added char marks from the wok."

Taste of India
- Address: 5517 Roosevelt Way NE, Seattle, WA 98105
- Price: $$$$
- Cuisine: Indian
- Perfect For: Casual Dinners, Sitting Outside, Birthdays
- Rating: 8.1
- Description: Half-block outdoor dining space with efficient service, complimentary samosas, and standout coconut curry with fish.

Off The Rez
- Address: 4300 15th Ave NE, Seattle, WA 98195
- Price: $$$$
- Cuisine: Native American
- Perfect For: Lunch, Cheap Eats
- Description: Burke Museum cafe serving Indigenous-centered cuisine featuring homemade frybread with various fillings.

Village Sushi
- Address: 5211 University Way Northeast, Seattle, WA 98105
- Price: $$$$
- Cuisine: Japanese
- Perfect For: Casual Dinners, Gluten-Free Options, Walk-Ins
- Description: Record-filled sushi restaurant with superior fish quality compared to nearby competitors.

Hiroshi's Poke
- Address: 4712 11th Ave NE, Seattle, WA 98105
- Price: $$$$
- Cuisine: Poke
- Perfect For: Cheap Eats, Serious Takeout Operation, Lunch
- Description: Takeout-only poke spot with "sock-rocking" bowls featuring melty lomi lomi salmon or soy-soaked tuna.

The Mountaineering Club
- Address: 4507 Brooklyn Avenue N.E., Seattle, WA 98105
- Price: $$$$
- Cuisine: American
- Perfect For: Drinking Great Cocktails, Drinks & A Light Bite, First Dates, Sitting Outside
- Description: Graduate Hotel bar with Eagle Scout-inspired design, patio skyline views, and camping-themed cocktails.

Aladdin Gyro-Cery
- Address: 4139 University Way NE, Seattle, WA 98105
- Price: $$$$
- Cuisine: Greek
- Perfect For: Big Groups, Casual Dinners, Cheap Eats, Late Nights, Lunch, Serious Takeout Operation
- Rating: 7.7
- Description: Falafel specialist with crispy offerings and tahini dipping sauce. Fast service suitable for takeout.

Queen Mary Tea Room
- Address: 2912 NE 55th St, Seattle, WA 98105
- Price: $$$$
- Cuisine: Bakery/Cafe
- Perfect For: Special Occasions, Unique Dining Experiences, Gluten-Free Options
- Description: Afternoon tea service with seasonal decor, tiered towers including tomato bisque shots, and gluten-free options.

Morsel
- Address: 5000 University Way NE Suite D, Seattle, WA 98105
- Price: $$$$
- Cuisine: American
- Perfect For: Breakfast, Brunch, Coffee & A Light Bite, Gluten-Free Options, Kids, Cheap Eats
- Description: Biscuit-focused breakfast spot with rotating daily flavors and housemade spreads. Spanish Fly sandwich recommended.
