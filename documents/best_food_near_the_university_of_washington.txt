# Best Food Near the University of Washington
Date: Jun 4, 2025

1. Thai Tom - Small Thai restaurant on the Ave known for Pad Thai and Spicy Basil Noodle with casual atmosphere
2. Agua Verde Café & Paddle Club - Waterfront Mexican eatery on Portage Bay featuring seafood paella
3. Portage Bay Café - Popular brunch spot with organic, locally sourced food and customizable pancake bar
4. Thai 65 - Thai restaurant specializing in curries and noodle dishes with generous portions
5. Sizzle & Crunch - Korean restaurant offering bibimbap, fried chicken, ramen, and Korean BBQ
6. Paseo Caribbean Food - Small eatery known for Caribbean-influenced sandwiches with generous portions
7. Big Time Brewery & Alehouse - Microbrewery operating since 1988 serving craft beers and pub fare
8. Musashi's - Japanese sushi restaurant with udon noodles and teriyaki chicken options
9. Schultzy's Sausage - University Drive spot offering bratwurst, chorizo, and Polish sausage sandwiches
10. Pagliacci Pizza - Seattle pizzeria since 1979 with classic and specialty pizza varieties
11. U:Don Fresh Japanese Noodle Station - Modern noodle house serving curry, beef, and tempura udon
12. Sushi Kappo Tamura - Upscale sushi restaurant with miso soup and tempura selections
13. Cafe Solstice - Cozy coffee shop offering beverages and baked goods
14. Tacos Chukis - Mexican restaurant featuring al pastor tacos cooked on a rotating spit
15. Araya's Place Vegan Thai - Established 1987, vegan Thai with courtyard seasonal dining
16. Xi'an Noodles - Authentic Asian cuisine with hand-pulled noodles and dumplings, takeout/delivery with outdoor seating
17. Ba Bar University Village - High-end Vietnamese restaurant with craft cocktails and outdoor seating
18. Din Tai Fung - Taiwan chain famous for Shanghai-style soup dumplings and noodles
19. Wataru - Authentic sushi restaurant with intimate bar and seafood from Tokyo
20. Dick's Drive-in - Seattle staple since 1954 serving burgers, fries, and milkshakes at affordable prices
21. Ray's Boathouse - Dockside fine dining restaurant featuring Pacific Northwest seafood with Shilshole Bay views
22. Terra Plata - Melrose Market - Upscale restaurant using locally sourced ingredients with rooftop deck
23. Lost Lake Cafe & Lounge - Cozy restaurant and bar offering breakfast, brunch, dinner, and late-night coffee
24. The Pink Door - Italian restaurant with truffle fries, grilled eggplant, and pasta dishes
