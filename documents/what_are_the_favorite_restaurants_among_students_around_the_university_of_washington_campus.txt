What are the favorite restaurants among students around the University of Washington campus?
From South to North on the Ave (and surrounds):
Agua Verde - Hidden gem but always packed.
Shultzys - IMO, way too greasy but good beer and a great place to watch a game.
Cafe Solstice - popular hangout spot, not much food.
Pho * - Always popular for pho, different people each have their favorites.
Alladins - Goto for post-drinking gyros.
Samurai Noodle - One of the newer places, excellent ramen.
Cafe on the Ave - another popular hangout spot, better food than Solstice but more crowded/loud.
Shalimar - Fancy setting, average food, high prices. Place you go if you want to impress someone.
Chipotle - Only good Mexican on the Ave proper.
Flowers - lunchtime vegetarian buffet.
Kais - reasonably upscale lounge with good bar food.
District Lounge - thus far, undiscovered gem. An oasis of class among it's low brow neighbors.
Bilbao - Odd little spanish restaurant tucked inside a movie theatre. Good food but you never think to go there.
Pagliaccis - Decent Pizzaria.
Thai Tom - only Thai worth going to on the Ave IMO.
Jack's Tapas, Mostly Chinese - most awesomely bizarre name, really authenic Chinese food.
Taste of India - The only Indian place my Indian friends would allow me to go to.
La Casa del Mojito (RIP) - by far the best place on the Ave, now sadly deceased and replaced by an (ugh!) vegan cafe.
I Love NY Deli - a bit off the Ave but an excellent NY style deli.
Blue Onion Bistro - oddly awesome diner food. Eerily quiet the one time I went there (7pm on a Friday night)
Die Bierstube - come for the company, stay for das boot, ignore the funky aroma that pervades the place.



On campus:
Orin's Place - the new cafe in Paccar Hall actually does pretty good food. They have one entree, one sandwich, one vegetarian option and one salad, all freshly prepared and all change regularly (daily?). Con: they only serve lunch from 11-2.
Parnassus - best coffee on campus, if you can stomach the hipster snobbery and high-falutin' attitude of the patrons & employees.

Near campus:
Orange King - burgers, burgers, burgers.
Schultzy's - best draught beer in the U-District, delicious sausages, crispy fries.
Flowers - food & tap selection reminiscent of Cap Hill. Vegetarian options.
